Campaign Agenda

Transparent Public Funds

Every rupee spent must be visible to the public with bills, work progress, and contractor details.

Water Security

Scheduled water supply, preventive maintenance, and solar-powered pumping systems.

Drainage Maintenance

Regular desilting and cleaning of blocked drainage canals before monsoon seasons.

Clean & Green Village

Tree plantation, compost systems, and scientific waste management.

Women Safety

CCTV cameras, proper street lights, emergency helplines, and safer public spaces.

Youth Empowerment

Digital literacy, skill training, e-library access, and entrepreneurship support.
